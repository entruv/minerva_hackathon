About Neck Wonder

Neck Wonder is your companion in your journey towards better posture and habits. We aim to help you build better habits by reminding you to do your stretches. These simple stretches take fewer than 3 minutes but can prevent a lifetime of bad posture and chronic back pain.

During the exercise, we track your head position with your webcam to help you do it right! Those exercises get converted into our currency â€” Posture Poins (PP). What can you get with Posture points? Goods, services & experiences ranging from anti-gravity yoga classes to high-tech shoes, to iPhones and Apple Watches. You can also donate your Sweatcoins to some of our amazing partnering charities, making for an easy way to help others around the world.

Don't know how to do these exercises? Our videos will guide you through them and help you start building better posture and health.


So what are you waiting for? Sign up and lets improve our postures together!

Video Demonstration

Here is how you do the exercise and earn Posture Points(PP):

  1. Sit relaxed in your neutral position
  2. Tuck your chin in and lean back from your shoulders and above
  3. Lean your head, neck and shoulders back as much as you can
  4. Feel the stretch in your back and the tension in your neck being relieved
  5. Repeat this action at your own time and pace 5 times
  6. Repeat these steps every 2 hours of computer desk use or when you feel tension in your neck
